Lingaraj Temple

The Lingaraj Temple is a magnificent example of Kalinga architecture and a major pilgrimage site in Odisha.

Dhauli Shanti Stupa

Dhauli is famous for its peace pagoda and Ashokan edicts, symbolizing Emperor Ashoka's transformation to Buddhism.

Sun Temple Konark

The Sun Temple in Konark is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, celebrated for its stunning architecture and intricate carvings.

Udayagiri and Khandagiri Caves

The Udayagiri and Khandagiri Caves are ancient Jain monuments with historical and archaeological significance.

ISKCON Temple

ISKCON Bhubaneswar is a spiritual center dedicated to Lord Krishna, attracting devotees from around the world.

Mukteshwar Temple

Mukteshwar Temple is known for its intricate carvings and is often referred to as the "Gem of Odisha's Temple Architecture."

Maa Kalijai

Maa Kalijai Temple, located on an island in Chilika Lake, is a sacred site for devotees and a popular tourist destination.
